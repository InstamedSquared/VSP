# VSP Workforce Management: Operational Flow & System Overview

Welcome to VSP Workforce Management. This document provides a high-level overview of how our platform streamlines the connection between clients, employees, and administrative operations. 

VSP is designed specifically for BPO and offshore staffing, ensuring transparency, productivity, and seamless financial management.

---

## 1. The Core Ecosystem

VSP operates as a centralized hub connecting three key groups:
*   **Clients:** Manage their dedicated offshore teams, view productivity, and handle billing.
*   **Employees:** Access their schedules, training, payslips, and profile information.
*   **VSP Administration:** Oversees recruitment, compliance, payroll, and overall operations.

---

## 2. The Client Experience

As a client, your journey with VSP is designed to be transparent and efficient.

### A. Staffing & Onboarding
*   **Hiring Requests:** Easily submit requests for new staff or specialized roles.
*   **Contract Repository:** Securely access all your agreements, including Master Service Agreements (MSA), NDAs, and HIPAA Business Associate Agreements (BAA).
*   **Training Integration:** Upload your own Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) or training videos (Loom, etc.) and assign them directly to your team members.

### B. Daily Operations & Monitoring
*   **Real-Time Productivity:** View attendance and activity levels synchronized directly from Hubstaff. This includes work hours, activity percentages, and (if enabled) screenshots.
*   **Leave Management:** Review and approve or reject leave requests from your assigned staff with a single click.
*   **Communication:** Receive important company-wide updates and announcements directly through your portal.

### C. Billing & Payments
*   **Automated Invoicing:** Receive professional PDF invoices automatically generated based on agreed rates (hourly or monthly), including any overtime or holiday pay.
*   **Secure Payments:** Pay invoices safely through our Stripe integration, with support for auto-pay and recurring billing.
*   **Financial History:** Access a full history of your receipts and billing statements at any time.

---

## 3. The Employee Journey

We ensure our workforce is well-supported and compliant, leading to better results for your business.

### A. Recruitment & Skills
*   **Rigorous Screening:** Every employee goes through a multi-stage recruitment process (Screening → Assessment → Interview).
*   **Skill Matching:** We maintain a detailed database of employee skills and certifications to ensure the right fit for your needs.

### B. Professional Development (LMS)
*   **Continuous Learning:** Employees have access to our Learning Management System (LMS) for essential training, such as HIPAA compliance, medical billing, or customer service excellence.
*   **Compliance Tracking:** We automatically track and remind employees about expiring certifications or required policy acknowledgments.

### C. Payroll & Benefits
*   **Transparent Payslips:** Employees can download their payslips and track their compensation and benefits through their personal dashboard.
*   **Reprofile System:** If an employee's current assignment ends, they enter our "Bench" system, where they are prioritized for new roles, ensuring we retain top talent.

---

## 4. Operational Excellence

VSP handles the "heavy lifting" of backend operations so you can focus on your core business.

*   **Compliance & Audit:** We manage HIPAA compliance, device security checks, and internet redundancy audits to ensure your data and operations remain secure.
*   **Hubstaff Synchronization:** Our deep integration with Hubstaff automates the tracking of hours, which feeds directly into both client invoicing and employee payroll, eliminating manual errors.
*   **Replacement & Coverage:** If you need to replace a staff member or require emergency coverage, our "Floating Bench" system allows us to rapidly reassign qualified personnel to your account.

---

## 5. Security & Data Privacy

We take data security seriously. VSP employs enterprise-grade security measures:
*   **Encrypted Data:** All sensitive information, including financial details and personal IDs, is encrypted.
*   **Access Control:** Strict role-based access ensures that only authorized individuals can view sensitive client or employee data.
*   **Audit Trails:** Every significant action in the system is logged, providing a clear audit trail for compliance and security purposes.

---

## Summary of the Flow

1.  **Request:** Client requests staff or submits a hiring requirement.
2.  **Match:** VSP matches the request with a qualified candidate from our pool or bench.
3.  **Onboard:** Contracts are signed, and training (SOPs) is assigned.
4.  **Work:** Employee logs hours via Hubstaff; Client monitors productivity.
5.  **Bill:** System auto-generates an invoice; Client pays via Stripe.
6.  **Pay:** VSP processes payroll and issues payslips to the employee.

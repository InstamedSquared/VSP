MODULE BREAKDOWN
1. CLIENT PORTAL - The Client Portal will serve as the client-facing operational dashboard where clients can manage their offshore staff, review billing, approve leaves, access contracts, and upload training materials.
	Client Dashboard
	- View active staff assigned to the client
	- Attendance and time tracking overview
	- Leave request approvals
	- Payroll and billing summaries
	- Access invoices and receipts
	- Access contracts and agreements
	- Company announcements and updates
	- Uploaded SOPs and training materials
	- Staff Management

	Clients can:
	- Approve or reject leave requests
	- Monitor attendance and Hubstaff activity
	- Review staff productivity reports
	- Request staff replacements
	- Request additional hires
	- Submit staff performance evaluations
	
	Billing Module
	- Automatic invoice generation based on:
	- Hourly rate
	- Monthly salary
	- Overtime
	- Holiday pay

	Benefits
	- Client-specific markups
	Example Invoice Breakdown [Staff, Hours, Rate, Total] -> [Maria Santos, 160, $10/hr, $1600]

	Billing Features
	- Auto-generated PDF invoices
	- Stripe payment integration
	- Payment history tracking
	- Due date reminders
	- Downloadable billing statements
	- Client Contract Repository

	Store and manage:
	- Master Service Agreements (MSA)
	- NDAs
	- HIPAA Business Associate Agreements (BAA)
	- Statements of Work (SOW)
	- Staffing agreements

	Future enhancement:
	- E-signature support
	- Training & SOP Uploads
	- Clients can upload:
	- SOP documents
	- Training PDFs
	- Loom or training videos
	- Onboarding guides
	- Workflow instructions
	- Training materials can be assigned directly to employees.


2. EMPLOYEE DASHBOARD - The Employee Dashboard will serve as the workforce portal where employees can access their information, payslips, training, schedules, and profiling status.
	Employee Profile
	Store and manage:
	- Resume
	- Government IDs
	- Emergency contacts
	- Contracts
	- Equipment assignment
	- Skills and certifications
	- HIPAA training records
	- Employment history
	- Payslip Module

	Employees can:
	- Download payslips
	- View payroll history
	- Track deductions
	- View bonuses and incentives
	- Review salary summaries
	- Employee Reprofile System

	Employees who become:
	- Floating
	- Partially available
	- Removed from a client
	- Open for reassignment
	- Can mark themselves as: “Available for Reprofile”

	Recruitment and Admin can:
	- View available employees first
	- Match employees to open client positions
	- Reduce downtime and staffing losses
	- Employee Resume Repository

	Employees can:
	- Update resumes
	- Upload certifications
	- Maintain employment documents
	- Store portfolios or work samples
	- Employee Courses / LMS

	Training and learning system for:
	- HIPAA Compliance
	- Dental terminology
	- Insurance verification
	- Medical billing
	- Customer service
	- Call handling
	- Internal VSP processes

	LMS Features
	- Course tracking
	- Quiz scores
	- Completion certificates
	- Training status monitoring
	- Required course assignments
	- Salary Disbursement Module
	Features:
	- Payroll summaries
	- Salary release tracking
	- Bank or wallet information
	- Payroll export support
	- Future integrations:
	- Wise
	- Payoneer
	- GCash
	- Direct bank transfer APIs
